Orthopedic Specialist

Dr Padmanabh Vora is an Ahmedabad-based Orthopaedic surgeon with over 9 years of national and international experience in Orthopaedic Surgery. His subspecialty interest is in field of Joint preservation, Joint replacement and Complex limb trauma wherein he has performed more than 1500 surgeries independently.

After finishing his post-graduation in Orthopaedics from renowned B.J. Medical College, Ahmedabad in 2016, he went to United Kingdom to pursue further specialty training. He worked in Tameside General Hospital, Greater Manchester and Hampshire Hospital, Basingstoke with world-renowned hip and knee surgeon teams. He cleared MRCS exams to become Member of Royal College of Surgeons, Edinburgh in 2020. He pursued research degree and achieved M.Ch in Orthopaedics from Edgehill University, Ormskirk in 2022

Dr Vora has worked in public sector hospitals on important positions well as private sector and corporate hospitals in India. He has rich work experience in NHS England over 5 years. He actively engages with various academic associations and societies to keep himself updated in Orthopaedics. Dr Vora strongly believes in high ethical standards, integrity and accountability. Over years, he has received great response and feedback from his professional colleagues, seniors and most importantly patients for his work.
