Knee Replacement Surgery

Knee replacement surgery, also known as knee arthroplasty, is a medical procedure designed to relieve pain and restore function in severely diseased knee joints. The damaged parts of the knee are replaced with artificial implants.

The procedure is commonly recommended for patients suffering from osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, or other degenerative joint diseases. With modern surgical techniques, knee replacement has a high success rate and provides long-term relief to patients.
